# Field-to-Proof Packet Factory Brief

Date: 2026-07-01

## Decision

Run the next 30 days as `field_to_proof_packet_factory_alpha`: a product
family, not a monolith. The first market-facing surface is the proof-packet
factory itself, with three public demos.

## Ranking

1. `AgentActionProofPacketFactory` for AI platform, safety, compliance, and infra teams.
2. `ResearchProofPacketFactory` for math labs, theory groups, AI proof teams.
3. `ClaimToExperimentPacketFactory` for AI4Science labs, biotech platform teams, translational research groups.

## Mechanism

The factory wins only if it preserves incumbent outputs while adding the missing
cross-layer packet: source receipts, workspace context, route decision, claim
graph, oracle/protocol, executable or formal branch, verifier verdict, negative
result lane, buyer brief, and non-promotion boundary.
