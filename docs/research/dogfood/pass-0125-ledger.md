# Pass 0125 Ledger: YouTube Experiment Router

Date: 2026-07-01

## Objective

Turn the four refreshed YouTube source leads into an executable experiment
router. The pass treats video metadata, transcript hashes, and derived keyword
signals as critical frontier-signal data, then routes that pressure into
falsifiable architecture and product experiments across Gather, Index, Forum,
Crucible, Telos, BuildLang/buildc, build-universe, and runtime receipts.

This pass does not verify the videos' scientific claims, expose raw transcripts
in packet artifacts, claim market fit, or promote a natural law.
