# Pass 0068 Steelman: Multi-Tool Growth Vectors

Date: 2026-07-01

Status: `LOCAL_STEELMAN_RECORDED`

The strongest objection to the megatool strategy is not that the pieces are
weak; it is that integration can hide the absence of one indispensable buyer
pain, one reproducible technical win, or one narrow wedge. This pass therefore
keeps every growth vector as a hypothesis and requires a falsifier before any
row can be promoted.

## Kill Criteria

- `market_pull` kill criterion: No buyer can name a must-have audit gap.
- `integration_cost` kill criterion: Adapters require raw private payloads to replay.
- `compiler_scope` kill criterion: No runtime receipt beats incumbent reproducibility workflows.
- `research_claim_quality` kill criterion: A packet cannot reject false or overstated claims.
- `routing_fragility` kill criterion: Broad Telos prompts still escalate after route fixtures are added.
- `receipt_fatigue` kill criterion: Replay confidence does not increase after adding receipts.
- `standards_overlap` kill criterion: A Telos packet cannot add claim verification or action authority beyond imports.
- `proof_overclaim` kill criterion: Any artifact promotes market uniqueness or scientific discovery without evidence.

Non-promotion statement: Pass 0068 ranks hypotheses and executable experiment designs. It does not prove market adoption, product completion, unique competitor absence, scientific discovery, or new natural laws.
